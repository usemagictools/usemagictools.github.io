How does the online calculator work without installation? ▼
Our All-in-One Calculator runs entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. It includes a scientific calculator with trigonometric functions (sin, cos, tan), logarithms, and factorials; a financial interest calculator for loans and investments; a subnet mask calculator for network engineers; and 16 types of unit conversion. Since everything is computed locally, there is zero latency, no server dependency, and your calculation data never leaves your device.
Can I use the Pomodoro Timer to improve my productivity? ▼
Absolutely. The Pomodoro Timer follows the classic Pomodoro Technique: work in focused 25-minute intervals separated by short 5-minute breaks, with a longer break after every four cycles. You can customize work duration, break length, and notification sounds. The timer works in your browser tab and can send desktop notifications so you never miss a break. It is a proven method used by millions to fight procrastination and maintain deep focus throughout the day.
Is the Password Generator secure enough for real accounts? ▼
Yes. The Password Generator uses the Web Crypto API (crypto.getRandomValues) built into modern browsers, which provides cryptographically secure random numbers. You can configure password length, include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Since the password is generated entirely on your device and never transmitted over the network, it is safe for banking, email, social media, and any other accounts. We recommend generating passwords of at least 16 characters for maximum security.
What is the Angel Number Calculator and how does numerology work? ▼
Angel numbers are repeating number sequences (like 111, 222, 333) believed to carry spiritual messages from guardian angels.
